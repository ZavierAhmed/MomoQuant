================================================================================
PRIMARY GOAL
================================================================================

Build a dedicated Validation Laboratory that determines whether a strategy has
robust historical edge on unseen data.

The Validation Laboratory must answer:

1. Does the raw strategy remain viable on candles that were not used to select
   or optimize its parameters?
2. Does performance collapse from training to validation?
3. Is the validation sample large enough to support a conclusion?
4. Are results dependent on one fragile parameter combination?
5. Do confidence and risk improve or damage the strategy on unseen data?
6. Are transaction costs responsible for apparent performance?
7. Is the strategy robust enough to move to Deployment Qualification?
8. Can the exact experiment be reproduced from immutable snapshots?

Core principle:

Strategy Laboratory:
"Does the implementation work, and what does each layer do?"

Validation Laboratory:
"Does the frozen strategy survive genuinely unseen historical data?"

The mandatory flow is:

Select data
    ↓
Create deterministic 70/30 chronological split
    ↓
Use only the first 70% for training and parameter selection
    ↓
Freeze strategy parameters and every dependent configuration
    ↓
Run once on the final unseen 30%
    ↓
Compare training versus validation
    ↓
Issue a robustness verdict

The unseen validation period must never influence training, optimization,
parameter selection, ranking, or threshold selection.
